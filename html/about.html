INTERSEX PHILIPPINES

Since its inception in 2019, the organization has developed the capacity to create secure and inclusive environments. It offers valuable resources and unwavering support that empower intersex Filipinos to embrace their identities, assert their rights, and actively engage in advocacy efforts. Furthermore, it has strategically collaborated with policymakers, lobbyists, and educators, driving meaningful policy changes while challenging the prevailing stigma and discrimination.

In a remarkably short span, Intersex Philippines has cultivated robust partnerships with diverse stakeholders, including LGBTIQ+ organizations, human rights groups, legal experts, and government bodies. This collaborative approach harnesses collective strength, propelling the organization closer to fulfilling its mission.

MISSION

To build and to support a community of empowered intersex individuals and families advocating for equal rights, self-determination and bodily autonomy.

VISION

A community of empowered intersex individuals and their families. Living with honor and dignity, forging an inclusive society

BOARD MEMBERS

Jeff Cagandahan

Chairperson and Executive Director

Jeff Cagandahan, the President and co-founder of Intersex Philippines, pioneers advocacy for the equality and protection of Filipino intersex individuals. As the first intersex Filipino legally allowed to change his sex marker in 2008, Jeff holds a significant role in advancing the rights of the intersex community. Beyond his leadership in Intersex Philippines, he serves as the co-chair of Intersex Asia contributing significantly to its growth and success. Jeff`s dedicated involvement, from the Executive Board to his current position, underscores his profound impact on the intersex movement in both national and regional contexts.
